Pin System — Inside Scoring Range Only
Green Pin
Attack the flag. Miss fat side.
Yellow Pin
Middle of green. Give yourself a putt.
Red Pin
Aim away from trouble. Bogey is fine.
70/30 Rule
Plan for your average shot, not your best. 70% of approaches should find the green safely.
Never Short-Side
A short-sided miss is the worst result on any hole. Miss fat side every time.

Round Mode

Work through each section in order before each shot. WELD calculates adjusted distance. Log Shot tracks your mental process using the C-C-E framework. Round Awareness tallies costly mistakes.

C-C-E Framework

Calculate (pre-shot plan) → Create (visualize the shot) → Execute (commit and swing). Log Y/N for each step after every shot.
